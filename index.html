About

Helping Small Businesses
Increase Profits

I help small business owners increase profits by an average of $7,500 per year by streamlining and simplifying their bookkeeping processes. Many entrepreneurs lose time and money managing disorganized finances or relying on inefficient systems. I step in to clean up, automate, and optimize financial workflows—giving you clearer insights, fewer errors, and more time to focus on growing your business.

My approach goes beyond basic bookkeeping. I look at how your financial systems actually function day-to-day, identify inefficiencies, and implement smarter processes that support long-term growth—not just short-term fixes.

Tools I Use
  • QuickBooks Online
  • Stripe & PayPal integrations
  • Excel / Google Sheets for custom reporting
  • Automation tools that reduce manual data entry

I choose tools based on what best fits your business—not a one-size-fits-all setup—so your systems stay simple, scalable, and cost-effective.

Why Choose True North

I’ve seen too many small business owners work incredibly hard, only to feel stressed and uncertain about their finances. Bookkeeping shouldn’t be a constant source of confusion or frustration—it should be a tool that helps you make confident decisions and grow your business. I do this work because I enjoy turning chaos into clarity. Helping you understand your numbers, improve your systems, and actually see the financial progress you’re working for is what drives me. When your books are in order, your business runs smoother—and that has a real impact on your time, your income, and your peace of mind.
